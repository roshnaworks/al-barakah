Our Story

Tradition Served with Honour

Al-Barakah — meaning "the blessing" — began as a humble family kitchen and has grown into a destination for bold South Indian non-veg cooking. Every dish carries recipes passed down through generations of Tamil Nadu kitchens.

From slow-cooked Chettinad mutton to fresh coastal seafood and biryani, we honour tradition with fresh ingredients, fiery spices, and generous hospitality.
